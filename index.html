My journey so far...

I'm a UK-based software engineer committed to leveraging technology for the betterment of society.

During my studies in Electronic Engineering, I found my calling in specialising in Computer Systems, I decided to dedicate my career to working with cutting-edge technologies that make a positive impact on people's lives and address global challenges.
I have worked on a variety of projects, from developing a automating a buisness-critical testing process for a leading media and entertainment company to developing software for next-generation robotic hands and systems with advanced dexterity to help solve challenging problems in the robotic automation and teleoperation industries.
Beyond coding, I'm an outgoing and inquisitive individual. I thrive on leading projects and guiding teams, fostering collaboration and creativity to deliver exceptional results. Embracing yoga and immersive travel experiences, I cultivate a balanced approach to life that fuels my drive to create innovative tech solutions and inspire meaningful change worldwide.
